Mining Sector Consultancy

Mining is an area which we have worked up on and that we acquired decades of experience. Ethiopia is very rich in having untapped mining resources and there are two areas of mining we consult. These are artisanal and modern mining. In artisanal mining we provide investors to work up on local quarries in line with legal mining frame work in regional local administrative places. We advise ho you can involve with local miners by bringing supportive equipment and technological knows. We advise you how you called prevent spillage of mineral products due to traditional method. So our consultancy helps the substituted mining products to be utilized economically and efficiently to their end purposes. Therefor by preparing a better background in local mining, we provide the way investors could get in local mining business In modern mining ventures, we consult investors in all mining work process from reconnaissance to prospective exploration and fill production, so that investors pay attention to their assessable work processes in production process. We provide advice to investors to work according to their capacity either at small medium and higher levels. Our consultancy process all permits and other legal duties expected of international and local investor. We provide legal advises as to how contracts are made both internationally and locally. Therefore our diligent and meticulous observations help the progress of investment in mining sectors. We help you to be license and work according to legal requirements.
